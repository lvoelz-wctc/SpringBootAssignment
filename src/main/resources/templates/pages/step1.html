On the eve following a thunderstorm, under the light of a crescent moon, travel 7 score and 7 times 7 paces beyond the northern Wisconsin Dunbar Pizzeria and Taxidermy office. Stray not off the path of stone, lest the benighted eyes of fell forest creatures cease their unyielding gaze to drag you into the unmarked wilds. Continue until you have reached the heart of the forest. It bears no marking fit for the eyes of humanity, but your soul shall sense the primal undulations of the unrestrained forest.

UPDATE 12/05/20: After feedback from the public, we have added a small fire pit, an outhouse, and a sign labeled "HEART OF THE FOREST."

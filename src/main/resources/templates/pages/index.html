Mission Statement

Here at Dunbar Pizzeria and Taxidermy, we know you love two things: Delicious pizza, and the ethereal sylvan climes of the Wisconsin Northwoods. But we've seen an unsettling trend over the last five years wherein attacks by fell beasts of the forest upon unsuspecting travelers have risen by 5000%. To this end, we are proud to announce a Dire Beasts as a Service (DBaaS) subscription where our customers can summon a personal Dire Beast for protection during their northwood ventures.

Due to unexpected demand, we are no longer able to provide Dire Wolves, Dire Bears, or Dire Owlbears. DBaaS subscribers may currently summon only Dire Possums. In the following pages, we've provided a convenient walkthrough that our clients may follow to summon their personalized Dire Possum at their leisure.

In the heart of the forest, position yourself directly below the North Star. Lay down an offering of trash from the nearest dumpster, light it on fire, and speak the following phrases:

"O MAGNA SED PARVAE MURE SILVARUM ALTA"

"SUSCIPE OBLATIONES MEAS QUISQUILIARUM"

"ET BENEDIC NOS TUA PRAESENTIA VISNE ULLAS"

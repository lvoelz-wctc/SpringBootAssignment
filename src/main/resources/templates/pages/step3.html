In the final step of the summoning process, the aspirant must best the Dire Possum in sword combat before it will submit to service as a summoned entity. While most aspirants will have widely varying preferred combat styles for fighting summoned entities, we've provided a list of recommendations that can be applied to most fighting methods.

  • Protect your eyes. Dire Possums are known to blind their opponents at the first chance.
  • Attempt a flurry of rapid strikes that can be easily redirected if needed. Dire Possums have exceptionally high agility and can easily dodge slow, telegraphed swings.
  • Do not attempt stealth. A Dire Possum can detect the scent of humans from miles away, and will likely counter your attempts with a sneak attack of its own.
